Project Description

This project aimed to better understand the relationship between latitude and common weather attributes. We explored this relationship among four attributes, temperature, wind, humidity, and cloudiness. On this website, you will have access to take a closer look at the cloudiness attribute and the temperature attribute.

You will find that there are interesting relationships between latitude and the attributes analyzed here. When viewing cloudiness and latitude in both the northern and southern hemisphere, you will identify that there is little to no correlation between these two variables. The r^2 value for both are below .10, indicating a poor fit of the linear regression line to the two variables relationship.

You will find a much more substantial relationship between latitude and the temperature attribute, in both the northern and southern hemisphere analyses, there was a much higher r^2 value, both above .45. While this is still not an ideal fit, it is more indicative that there a correlation exists between the two variables, temperature and latitude. ENJOY EXPLORING!
